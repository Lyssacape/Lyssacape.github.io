Research Experience

Next Generation Project

Folding Matters

Collaboratively worked with interdisciplinary engineers to design a self-operating collapsible structure as well as effectively delegated research topics to team members and lead team activities to achieve project goals in a timely and competitive fashion.

The design my team and created was a collapsable and expandable structure that could be delivered by companies such as FEMA or Red Cross in certain areas to create shelters for those who lost theirs in a disaster. Our idea was to have a 10' by 10' design that could expand into 18' in length to house up to 2 small families at a time. We created a protoype that was complete with technology similar to a scissor lift to expand out the sides. It was also complete with hinges that allowed the walls to expand outwards to make sure there was no area left uncovered.
